the story

Small things, thoughtfully made.

The porch goose is a beloved piece of Americana — a quiet, charming tradition of giving your outdoor space a little personality. The Mini Porch Goose brings that same warmth indoors, to the spots where you spend your quietest, happiest moments: beside a stack of library books, next to a candle on the windowsill, among the houseplants on the sill. Each one is printed and painted by hand in small batches in Ballard, Seattle, Washington, with the same care you'd give a handmade gift.

What is your shipping & return policy?

We ship anywhere in the US for a flat $5. Orders over $35 ship free. Allow 3–5 business days for production, plus standard USPS shipping (typically 3–7 days). If you're not happy with your goose for any reason, we accept returns within 30 days of delivery — just reach out to miniporchgoose@gmail.com and we'll sort it out.

How big is the Mini Porch Goose exactly?

It stands approximately 7 inches tall — roughly the height of a small candle or thick paperback. Sturdy and present, but not oversized.

What is it made from?

Each goose is 3D printed in PLA and hand-painted with acrylic. Best kept indoors away from prolonged moisture.

How long does it take to arrive?

Made to order — please allow 3–5 business days for production, plus standard USPS shipping (typically 3–7 days within the US).

Can I request a different colour or style?

Not at this time — the Mini Porch Goose comes in one classic hand-painted design. Follow along for news about seasonal or limited editions.

Do you ship outside the US?

US orders only for now. International shipping may be added in the future — feel free to reach out if you're outside the US and interested.
